The IMPATIENT MRI toolkit implements massively parallel MRI reconstruction methods on GPUs, including Bayesian reconstruction techniques using Markov Random Fields to impose spatial smoothness.

**Link:** [github.com/JiadingGai/Impatient-MRI](https://github.com/JiadingGai/Impatient-MRI).
